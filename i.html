Andhra Pradesh

Known for its rich culture, historical sites, and beautiful beaches. The state capital is Amaravati.

Arunachal Pradesh

Famous for its stunning landscapes, biodiversity, and tribal culture. It is home to the Tawang Monastery.

Assam

Renowned for its tea plantations, rich wildlife, and the Brahmaputra River. Kaziranga National Park is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Bihar

Known for its historical significance and ancient universities like Nalanda. The state is the birthplace of Buddhism.

Chhattisgarh

Known for its natural resources and tribal culture. The state is rich in forests and waterfalls.

Goa

Famous for its beaches, Portuguese heritage, and vibrant nightlife. It's a popular tourist destination.

Gujarat

Known for its diverse culture, handicrafts, and the Gir National Park, home to Asiatic lions. The state has a rich history and is the birthplace of Mahatma Gandhi.

Haryana

Known for its agriculture and rapid industrialization. It has a vibrant cultural heritage and is close to Delhi.

Himachal Pradesh

Famous for its picturesque mountains, hill stations, and adventure tourism. Shimla is the state capital.

Jharkhand

Known for its mineral resources and dense forests. It is home to several waterfalls and tribal communities.

Karnataka

Famous for its IT industry, rich heritage, and diverse landscapes. The capital, Bengaluru, is known as the Silicon Valley of India.

Kerala

Renowned for its backwaters, Ayurvedic treatments, and lush greenery. It's a popular destination for ecotourism.

Madhya Pradesh

Known for its historical monuments, national parks, and rich wildlife. It is home to the UNESCO World Heritage Sites of Khajuraho.

Maharashtra

Famous for its bustling cities like Mumbai, rich culture, and diverse cuisine. The state has a vibrant film industry (Bollywood).

Manipur

Known for its rich cultural heritage, classical dance, and beautiful landscapes. It’s famous for the Loktak Lake.

Meghalaya

Renowned for its living root bridges and waterfalls. The state has a rich tribal culture and is one of the wettest places on Earth.

Mizoram

Known for its scenic beauty, vibrant culture, and music. The state is home to various tribes and lush hills.

Nagaland

Famous for its tribal heritage, festivals, and crafts. The Hornbill Festival showcases the state’s rich culture.

Odisha

Known for its temples, dance forms, and beaches. The Konark Sun Temple is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Punjab

Famous for its agricultural productivity, rich history, and vibrant culture. Amritsar is home to the Golden Temple.

Rajasthan

Known for its royal heritage, forts, and desert landscapes. Jaipur, the Pink City, is a major tourist attraction.

Sikkim

Renowned for its natural beauty, biodiversity, and Buddhist culture. It offers stunning views of the Himalayas.

Tamil Nadu

Famous for its temples, classical music, and cuisine. Chennai is the state capital and a major cultural hub.

Telangana

Known for its rich history, cultural diversity, and cuisine. Hyderabad is famous for its biryani and IT sector.

Tripura

Known for its cultural diversity and beautiful landscapes. The state has several historical sites and temples.

Uttar Pradesh

Famous for its historical significance, including the Taj Mahal in Agra. It is a cultural and religious hub.

Uttarakhand

Renowned for its natural beauty, spirituality, and adventure sports. The state is home to several Hindu pilgrimage sites.

West Bengal

Known for its art, literature, and cultural festivals. Kolkata, the capital, is famous for its colonial architecture and vibrant culture.
